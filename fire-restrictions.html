Fire Restrictions

Current Status: LEVEL 2 – Severe Restrictions (No open burning, fireworks, or outdoor fires permitted.)

What LEVEL 2 means for you

  • No campfires, fire pits, or chimineas — even if you have a permit.
  • No charcoal grills or wood-burning stoves outdoors. Gas grills are allowed at private residences.
  • All fireworks and pyrotechnics are strictly prohibited.
  • No smoking in open spaces, parks, or trails. Smoking is allowed only inside buildings or enclosed vehicles.
  • No outdoor welding or use of open-flame torches.
  • Chainsaw operation requires a spark-arresting device and nearby fire extinguisher.

Fire Restriction Levels

  • Level 1: Limited burning in designated areas only.
  • Level 2: Complete outdoor burning ban in effect.
